generated by the MARS2000 tool developed by Bureau Veritas (
http://www.veristar.com).
Bureau Veritas cannot be held responsible for the use of this file made by
the user.
The users are reminded that scantlings resulting of the LBR5 output results
are to be checked by Bureau Veritas for classification or certification
